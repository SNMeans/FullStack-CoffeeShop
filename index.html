Our Mission

Our faith-based approach and commitment to community embody the three C's: Connection,Conversation, & Caffeination. Whether you're seeking a moment of solitude with your favorite brew or craving the company of fellow coffee enthusiasts, there's always a seat waiting for you at Flo's !

Brews On The Move

Whether you're on campus, at a local market, or celebrating a special event, we're here to caffeinate your day and brighten your spirits. Join us for a sip of joy on the go – because great coffee and good vibes go hand in hand!
